Terms and condition

  • Points will be earned on anonymous, personalized and registered Nol Cards only
  • Anonymous cards get 1 point for every 2 AED spent
    • Points are awarded once the card is linked to a Nol+ account
  • Personalized/ Registered cards get 1 point for every 1 AED spent
    • Points are awarded from August 1st, 2018
  • You will be earning Nol Plus points when you use your Nol card in public transportation, RTA parking meter, paying RTA fines, public parks, Etihad Museum, Happiness centres fuel and shopping in Mercury outlets and restaurants
  • Your Nol Plus points will be visible after 9 working days from the date of usage
  • Your Nol Plus points will be valid for 12 months
  • Your Nol+ points are linked to your account, so even if any of your cards expire your awarded points will still be available on your account
  • You can link up to 4 cards to your account
  • You can still earn points on expired cards until their credit is 0 AED, your Nol+ points will be available on your account after card expire
  • Once the anonymous card is delinked, the points will no longer be rewarded upon usag
  • AED 1 is the lowest earning increment
  • Redemption value, 1 point = 0.01 AED
  • Minimum redemption threshold is 300 points an equivalent to AED 3
